Diff, born in the 1980s in Changsha, Hunan. Christian, designer, husband to one woman, father of three children. Entered the internet world during university in 2001, fascinated with web design and Flash animation, and fortunate to experience the most open era of the Chinese internet. Transitioned to app design in 2009, becoming one of China's earliest mobile internet designers. From 2013 to 2019, as the 7th employee and first designer, I was fully involved in Liulishuo's journey from a startup in a residential home to its IPO on the NYSE (NYSE: LAIX). Previously lived in Hangzhou and Shanghai. Now residing in Dallas, Texas, USA.

### Timeline

- **2005**, Graduated from Central South University with a major in Art and Design.
- **2006**, Started living in Hangzhou, joined a family church in Hangzhou after two months (a personal experience of God's leading me to "leave my homeland").
- **2008**, Baptized and committed to Christ.
- **2006 to 2013**, Joined various startup companies, always at the forefront of the industry, worked in online travel, video websites, music websites, and mobile OS. Had a year of independent entrepreneurship, but it ended with my personal withdrawal.
- **May 4, 2013**, Joined the Liulishuo startup team as the 7th employee, moving to Shanghai with the team.
- **September 2018**, Liulishuo went public on NASDAQ.
- **May 4, 2019**, Departed from Liulishuo. In the past six years, had led a design team of over 50 people and a children-oriented Liulishuo business team of around 140.
- **2019 to 2021**, Stayed at home, doing some design consultancy projects as a hobby.
- **January 2021**, Joined a special team as a designer.
- **August 8, 2022**, Immigrated to the United States, currently settled in Los Angeles.
- **August 8, 2024**, Moved to Dallas.
- **March 30, 2026**, Became an independent developer without coding skills.
