> Genesis 29:15-20
> 15: Laban said to Jacob, "You are my own flesh and blood. Should you work for me for nothing? Tell me what your wages should be."
> 18: Jacob was in love with Rachel and said, "I'll work for you seven years in return for your younger daughter Rachel."
> 20: So Jacob served seven years to get Rachel, but they seemed like only a few days to him because of his love for her.

When there is someone worthy of love, there is passion, and one does not dwell on gains and losses beyond that beloved.

Jacob served seven years for Rachel, and it seemed like only a few days. As for me, what am I "serving" or working for?

What do I love?
