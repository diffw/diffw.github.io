> Genesis 29:15-20
> 15 Laban said to him, "Just because you are a relative of mine, should you work for me for nothing? Tell me what your wages should be."
> 18 Jacob was in love with Rachel and said, "I'll work for you seven years in return for your younger daughter Rachel."
> 20 So Jacob served seven years to get Rachel, but they seemed like only a few days to him because of his love for her.

When there's someone worth loving, passion follows, and you won't worry about gains and losses outside of that love.

Jacob served seven years for Rachel, which felt like only a few days. What am I serving for and working towards now?

What is it that I love?
