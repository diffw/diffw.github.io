Originally, I started a WeChat public account to create opportunities for "writing." But in reality, I wrote very little. The main reasons are that picking up a pen amidst a busy work schedule is too hard; explaining something clearly is too hard; writing an article that resonates and receives likes and compliments is too hard. So, gradually, I gave up.

Last week, I suddenly had an idea to write for "five minutes" each day. I set a "five-minute" timer. The ticking sound pushed me to think quickly and jot down notes rapidly. It was very effective, and now I can write again.

I recently moved house. I went from a "ten-minute" commute to a "one and a half hour" journey, but strangely enough, I'm now punctual for work. Haha, people are really interesting, aren't they?
