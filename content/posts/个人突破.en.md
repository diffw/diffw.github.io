Presentation has always been my weak spot.  
M said I don't lack eloquence or confidence, and I agree.  
What I lack is the ability to convey confidence and information to the audience.

Honestly, when I think about this, the truth is there's a deep-seated insecurity within me.  
It binds me, preventing clear expression.  
But recently, I broke through by presenting to a top boss.  
Suddenly, a lot of things became clear.

It's a memorable day.  
(I vaguely sense God's help in my heart, hoping to experience it more clearly)
