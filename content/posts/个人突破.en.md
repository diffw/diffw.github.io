presentation has always been my weakness.
M says that I don't lack eloquence or confidence, and I agree.
What I lack is how to convey confidence and information to the audience.
Honestly facing this question, at the root,
I have a deep sense of inferiority within me,
which binds me and prevents me from expressing myself accurately.
But recently, through giving a presentation to a top leader, I broke through.
All of a sudden, many things became clear to me.
It was a day worth commemorating.
(Inwardly, I vaguely feel the help of God, and I hope to experience it more clearly)
