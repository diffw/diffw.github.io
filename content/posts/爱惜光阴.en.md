Sometimes, at work, I have a tendency to just "pass the time." Why take life so seriously?

However, after seeing the stories and works of creators who shine brightly after their struggles, I am filled with deep regret.

If this moment is my choice, then I should give it my all; or, should I take another path? In any case, I need to think it through.

The most precious thing for a person is time. "Passing the time" means wasting one's own "life." I don't want to live that way. Besides, as a Christian, I work in the presence of the Lord. How dare I slack off in front of the Creator?

> Teach us to number our days, that we may gain a heart of wisdom.
> Psalm 90:12

> Make the most of every opportunity, because the days are evil. Therefore do not be foolish, but understand what the Lord’s will is.
> Ephesians 5:16-17
