### Past Reflections
Looking back at the plans from 2007, 2009, and 2010, few of the items were accomplished. Some plans were forgotten almost as soon as they were made.

If plans are so unreliable, then what is the significance of planning? Is it a plan or a wishlist? Nowadays, making a plan feels like making a wish—casual, without the need for execution or accountability. Of course, the Bible also teaches us not to make vows lightly.

--

The original intentions for making a plan:

- A life without a plan is chaotic. This is clearly not God's way, as His creation of heaven and earth was so planned and orderly.
- Plans provide direction in life. (Daily life is like navigating at sea, full of tempting fog and easy to lose direction and be indecisive.)
- Plans make life more efficient and secure, allowing better enjoyment. (Without a financial plan, family finances become out of control; you could have saved for a travel fund to go abroad, but without a plan, you lose the opportunity to enjoy life.)

--

Why do plans fail?

- Motivational issues, not realistic
- Plans are casually made without careful consideration
- Lack of execution and persistence
- Realistically, not all plans will succeed

--

A complete and effective plan includes:

- Goals and motivation (Goals first, then plans; motivations that please God) (If the Lord wills, we will live and do this or that)
- Action steps (How will I do it?)
- Detailed plan (What will I do?)

--

What areas to plan for?

- Spiritual life
- Finances
- Work
- Education
- Entertainment

For men with families, it also includes:

- Family
- Children's education
- Parent support

For Christians, it also includes:

- Church service
- Evangelism

----

What are your values? What are your goals? What is most important to you? Is everything you are doing really related to these? Is it really indispensable? Can it not be done by someone else? Can you use some tools to simplify it (such as setting up email filters)? Think about all the things you plan to do; you might find you don't need to stay so busy.

----
