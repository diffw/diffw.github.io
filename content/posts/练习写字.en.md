I've realized for quite some time that I'm not good at expressing myself, whether through words or graphics. I envy others who can articulate their thoughts clearly and effortlessly.

But what's the use of envy? I might as well practice.

When it comes to my blog, I used to think that only valuable content should be shared to make it worthwhile for readers. After all, who needs to hear someone rambling about trivial daily matters?

Truth is, I know I don't have many readers. So, I became resigned.

I've decided to treat this space as a place for casual expression. As for any projects or artworks I occasionally create, I'll be moving them to [www.hulitu.com](http://www.hulitu.com) over time.

Additionally, I'd like to practice writing in English. If you notice any word choice or grammatical errors, please forgive me and feel free to point them out in the comments. Thank you. I'm a beginner.
