In my early morning prayer, I thought again of that colleague. Once during lunch, he mentioned to me, "I can't finish my work. I need to work overtime even on Saturdays." At the time, I simply responded with an "Oh." Now I realize I shouldn't have been so indifferent.

God has actually provided many opportunities to "win his soul," but I was too careless and indifferent. After giving a presentation on "marriage relationships" at the company, a colleague left a comment saying they were "almost ready to join the faith for the second time." I should have followed up more.

Lord, please keep me vigilant and guide me to be your mouthpiece.

> Preach the word; be prepared in season and out of season; correct, rebuke and encourage—with great patience and careful instruction. 
> 2 Timothy 4:2
