Lack of Creativity
The mind is filled with mundane matters, leaving no room for creative pursuits.
The body is becoming increasingly sluggish,
Gradually turning into nothing more than a food processing machine.

A change is needed.

Focus your energy on creativity, design, and their value.
Be passionate, dare to imagine and act (otherwise, in middle age, I would really regret not having ventured out for a try).

Start exercising to rejuvenate the body (like an eagle rejuvenating in spring).

Be more confident.
