Lack of creativity.

My mind's filled with trivial matters, leaving no room for creativity.

Getting physically sluggish.

Turning into a food-processing machine.

Need to change.

Focus, concentrate on creativity, design, and their value.

Get passionate, think big and act boldly (or I'll really regret not stepping up when I hit middle age).

Start exercising, get my body back in shape (like an eagle renewing its youth).

Be a bit more confident.
