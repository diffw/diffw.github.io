As is well known, rhinitis is still a stubborn condition that the medical community has not yet been able to solve. I am one of those who suffer greatly from rhinitis, including being awakened around 1 a.m. every day by sneezing or a runny nose. This situation has persisted for over a year, and it gets worse in the winter.

Imagine facing this every day; now imagine living like this for decades to come. I was desperate until one night I let out a long, frustrated shout of "Why is it like this?!!!"

However, I'm a Christian. The God I trust is the one who created the heavens and the earth from the beginning, the only God. Doesn't Jesus have authority? Didn't He rise from the dead? With these thoughts, I said a simple prayer before bed last night, asking Jesus to protect my nose and my sleep.

In the middle of the night, my nose itched several times, and I almost sneezed. In this half-asleep, half-awake state, I called out, "Jesus, Jesus."

When I woke up in the morning, and even now, I haven't had a runny nose or sneezed. I want to thank God.

I don't know what tonight or tomorrow will bring, but today, I truly thank God. Of course, I'm someone who easily forgets God's grace, so I'm writing this down so I won't forget.
