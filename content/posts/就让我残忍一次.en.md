Perhaps I should have made this decisive choice long ago. Although there is much regret after the fact, and I even wanted to seek comfort and release by confiding in friends, rationally speaking, this is a good thing for both of us. Selfishly speaking, it is a good thing for me; I can completely leave 2005 behind and no longer let it have the slightest impact on my current life. It's a sign of my lack of strength. I hope you are well. 

Lately, and for the past six months, life has been terrible. One question I've been pondering constantly is: what is the purpose of living? Although my family, friends, and colleagues are great and my situation is not bad, I always feel incredibly empty. 

I am certain I can find the only answer through faith, through Christ, but my stubborn nature keeps me from submitting to God.

The sinning continues.

What kind of life should I lead? What is the direction of my life? How should I continue living this way?

I'm sorry. May we both find the strength we need. May we one day be able to return to a simple, peaceful friendship.
