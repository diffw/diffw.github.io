Today I got in touch about the student loan I've been postponing to repay.

The loan from around 2004 was for 12,000. To my surprise, with interest, it now amounts to 17,900.

The loan could have been repaid in installments, and if I had done so, the interest wouldn't have been so high.

There's nearly 6,000 more in interest.

I can't blame anyone else. I have to bear this cost myself, as it's a result of my own personality.
