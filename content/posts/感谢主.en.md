Thank You, Lord, for letting me live to this day;
Thank You, Lord, even though my neck and spine ache, I am still healthy and strong;
Thank You, Lord, although there is financial pressure with the child's imminent arrival, I still have no lack, with enough food and clothing;
Thank You, Lord, my parents are still healthy;
Thank You, Lord, though my wife and I have minor disagreements, we still love each other dearly;
Thank You, Lord, for knowing You, giving me hope when I'm happy and when I'm disappointed.
This morning's prayer, a brief record...
