Thank God, I'm alive today;
Thank God, my neck and back hurt, but I'm still healthy and strong;
Thank God, though there's financial stress with a baby on the way, I'm not lacking anything, I have enough to eat and wear;
Thank God, my parents are still healthy;
Thank God, my wife and I have small disagreements, but we still love each other dearly;
Thank God, I know you—hope when happy, hope even when disappointed.
Here's this morning's prayer, just jotting it down...
