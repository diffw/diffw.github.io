As an outstanding designer, the thing he feels he lacks the least—and values the least—is creativity. Even though a designer may not highly value their own creativity, it’s still worth a significant price for others to obtain. Yet, I often feel a scarcity of "creativity" and tend to overly cherish my so-called creativity. It seems I’m not quite outstanding yet. Let’s strive to make creativity cheap.
