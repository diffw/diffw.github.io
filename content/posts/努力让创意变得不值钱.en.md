As a badass designer, what he's least lacking, and what he considers least valuable, should be creativity. Even if a designer's creativity is the least valuable, it's still worth others paying a high price for it. But I often feel a "lack" of creativity and tend to overvalue my so-called creativity. Seems like I'm not badass enough. Let's work to make creativity worthless.
