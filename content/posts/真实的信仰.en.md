Quiet in the night, while showering, a strong feeling arose from deep within—a renewed faith, a choice to walk toward God once more, bringing me great peace and confidence.
This feeling is very real, and for the first time, I feel the authenticity of faith. I can't deny this truth, even though I have denied it countless times before.
God, I love you.
