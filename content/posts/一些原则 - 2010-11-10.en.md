If you think someone is incompetent, then present your own solution. Otherwise, keep quiet! Don't gossip like a busybody.
If you have an idea, don't share it publicly until you can articulate it clearly. Conveying incomplete thoughts to others, especially to your boss, may cause a good idea to fail due to lack of clarity.
If you can prove your own impressive capabilities while those around you are lackluster, then leave and seek better partners.
