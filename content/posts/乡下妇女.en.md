At a temporary train station in Xiaoshan, waiting for K137. The hall was packed with people.

Unexpectedly, an empty seat appeared in front of me, so I sat down.

Looking down, I saw a ground covered with sunflower seed shells.

It turns out that next to me sat a woman snacking.

She had yellowish skin, her hair was a bit messy and probably greasy. Her lifeless eyes mechanically stuffed food into her mouth.

Her hands were large and rough, with blackened nails.

Her shoes were dirty, and her socks were pilling.

She was peeling an orange. Casually and thoughtlessly, she tossed the peel onto the floor.

A rural woman!

How crude, how vulgar this rural woman is, I thought to myself.

I even secretly rolled my eyes at her in disdain and turned my head away.

After a short while, I couldn't help but glance at her again.

Hey, out of nowhere, I suddenly felt she was so distinctive, like a very unique supporting character arranged by a director in a movie.

Simple, direct, and so fearless; in a way, even a little endearing.
