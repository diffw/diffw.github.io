Ask less of life;
Ask less of others;
Control your desires and dreams. Focus on one thing at a time. (If it's two or three things, it's not really focus);
Things that require no commitment, and have no difficulty or challenge, hold no value. For example, watching TV, aimlessly browsing the web, or checking social media.
Don't be too greedy.

Two recent favorite quotes:
Feel excited every day to discover how little you know.
There are already enough smart people in the world; I'd rather be a fool.
