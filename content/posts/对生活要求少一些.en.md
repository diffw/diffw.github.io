Ask less of life;  
Ask less of others;  
Keep your desires and dreams in check. Focus on one thing at a time. (If it's two or three things, it's not called focus.)  
No need to invest in things without difficulty or challenge—they hold no value. For example, watching TV or mindlessly browsing the internet and Weibo.  
Don't be too greedy.

Two phrases I've liked recently:  
Excited to discover my ignorance every day.  
The world has enough smart people; I'd rather be a fool.
