The hectic life has finally come to a halt. If I kept going at that pace, even my wife would have had complaints.

Saturday, with crisp autumn weather. A few friends from church gathered at the Hangzhou Botanical Garden. Hangzhou is a city filled with scenic beauty; not knowing how to appreciate and enjoy it would truly be a waste.

Everyone was completely relaxed, and we had plenty of snacks and drinks. After the guys had eaten and drunk to their satisfaction, they headed to the water to catch crawfish.

After the group activity ended, the teacher felt it wasn't enough, and his wife said, "We haven't played enough," so we went to play billiards.

When there are no tigers in the mountain, the monkey becomes king. Among my college classmates, my billiard skills were terrible; now, I'm an expert.

However, I must admit I was pretty lucky, winning from start to finish. Also, I enjoy making power shots.

Haha, my little baby is so cute now and can interact with us. Every time I gently press on the spot where the baby is in the belly and let go, after two or three seconds, the baby responds with a little jump. It's very interesting.
