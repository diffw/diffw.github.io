Sometimes I am gentle and kind;  
Sometimes I am cunning and evil;

Sometimes, I love carrots;  
Sometimes, I adore hens;

I want to gain more;  
I also want to give less;

I hope every rabbit is my friend,  
Yet in my heart, I want to make all rabbits my dinner;

Stirring up storms in the fox den, doing bad things,  
Occasionally, I want to be a rabbit;

My heart leans toward God,  
But sometimes I am bound by Satan;

I yearn for redemption,  
But often I am self-centered, looking down on the heavens;

Very conflicted,  
I guess I'll always be this conflicted,  
Until the end of life;  
Until the day I face the Most High.

A fox rabbit;  
Small and real.
