I've decided to leave the company at the end of the year, in this season full of "harvests." :P 

To say the past year has been like a shadowy valley is no exaggeration. But I'm thankful to God for guiding my path and granting me faith and peace. It assures me that life's journey is prepared and aided by the Lord.

With peace, faith, and love in my heart, the world indeed feels wonderful. 

I love trees, flowers, sunlight, the night, the wind, mountains, flowing water, people running, beautiful girls, and playful children. I love all of life. When the wind comes, enjoy the wind; when the rain comes, enjoy the rain; when happiness comes, enjoy happiness; when suffering comes, enjoy suffering.

In the days to come, I will be reflecting and looking for new opportunities.

I'm going back to design; it feels like a return. Haha.
