I decided to leave this company at the end of the year, which is a season full of "harvests." :P

No exaggeration to say, this past year has felt like walking through the valley of the shadow of death. But thank God for guiding my path, giving me faith and peace. It assures me that life's journey is prepared and aided by the Lord.

When there's peace, faith, and love in your heart, the world truly feels beautiful.

I love trees, flowers, sunlight, the night, the wind, mountains, streams, running people, beautiful girls, and playful children. I love all life. When the wind comes, enjoy the wind; when the rain comes, enjoy the rain; when happiness comes, enjoy happiness; when hardship comes, enjoy hardship.

In the days ahead, I'll be summarizing and looking for a new job.

I'm going back to doing design again, which feels like returning home. Hehe.
