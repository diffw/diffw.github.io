Dear Brothers and Sisters:

My child is about 50 days away from being born. The baby is active and healthy. Thank you all for your prayers; the Spirit of God is protecting us.

Whenever I read scriptures about Eli's two sons, [Samuel](http://baike.baidu.com/view/128906.htm)'s sons, I often feel worried, so I ask for your prayers. :)

Let this child (confirmed not twins) rely wholeheartedly on the Lord and live a life that pleases Him.

Eli's two sons, Samuel's sons, seemed to be surrounded by an abundance of love from brothers and sisters who initially loved them unconditionally, but later couldn't keep up when things worsened. It's too late by then! Please pray diligently and early. People harbor evil thoughts from a young age!

For the children of colleagues, let's collectively ensure that we do not spoil them from the womb:)

HG has instructed me to make sure everything is ready and to spare you any concern over our lack by giving us gifts. It's important not to cultivate the habit of giving gifts easily to children from the womb. Let’s start there. Previously, America's most famous TV evangelist JIM BAKKER recalled after being released from prison that he was very chubby because kind uncles and aunts at the church kept buying him delicious things, which had a significant impact on his life. His son grew up in the greenhouse of the church but found it really challenging to adapt when he entered society. Please ensure to watch over your loving intentions to avoid them causing a child to stumble. Pray earnestly but refrain from giving us material gifts.
