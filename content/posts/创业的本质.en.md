This process can easily get puffed up.

It seems another social network is about to be born or that some model is about to be disrupted yet again; talking about user numbers, open platforms, investment, and profit margins. It seems like passion, but it's already slipped into a kind of blindness and illusion.

"Is it really bringing value to users? Does it meet a hard or a soft need of theirs?" An entrepreneur or a designer needs to ask themselves this often.

Having dreams or ambitions is a good thing, but keep these close to your heart, guard them fiercely. Then, humbly, quietly, and solidly, get things done.
