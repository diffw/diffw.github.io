This process can easily become superficial. 
It seems like another social network is about to emerge, or a model is being disrupted; 
Talking about user numbers, open platforms, investments, and profits; 
It appears to be passion, yet it has already fallen into a blind illusion.  

"Does it really bring value to users? Does it meet their hard demands or soft desires?" 
Entrepreneurs and designers need to frequently ask themselves this question.  

Having dreams or ambitions is a good thing, but keep them deeply in your heart, guarding them closely. 
Then, humbly, modestly, and solidly, get things done.
