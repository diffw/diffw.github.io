Until today, at the age of 28, I've finally come to understand a truth:
> "The real world will never fully satisfy you."

Whether it's the United States, viewed by many as the "land of freedom and dreams," or Taiwan, or China. So, sometimes, I really yearn for that "kingdom of heaven" mentioned in the Bible.

As a designer, after five years in the professional world, what I've come to realize is:
> "Dissent and controversy will always exist, unless you're dealing with beings without thought.
> If you can convince others, you should feel fortunate; if not, there’s no need to complain or be disappointed.
> 'Others' refers to people who are different from you.
> Moreover, there are times when even you are unsure about certain things."
