When you're uncertain about a single task, or facing a problem that logic can't solve, trust your intuition.

To be honest, I have always been someone who didn't believe in intuition. I thought people who acted on their gut were unreliable and rash. After experiencing a period where I acted purely on instinct, followed by a phase where reason and logic gradually took over, and finally a time when reason and logic contradicted each other, I've come to realize that intuition is the most genuine.
