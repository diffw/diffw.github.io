When you're undecided on something specific or logic fails to solve a problem, trust your gut.

Honestly, I've always been someone who didn't believe in intuition, thinking that relying on gut feelings was unreliable and rash.

After going through a period of acting purely on feeling, then a time where feelings were mostly replaced by reason and logic, and finally, a stage where reason and logic clashed entirely, I now believe intuition is the most genuine.
