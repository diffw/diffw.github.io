1999 to 2014 (2 years ago), then to 2025 (9 years ahead), feels like being in the movie, as memories from the past flood in.

A lifetime truly passes in a blink. Such a brief moment, yet filled with all kinds of tastes, daunting to savor, yet often reminisced.

One day, will I return to the mountains and rivers, and meet old friends again?
