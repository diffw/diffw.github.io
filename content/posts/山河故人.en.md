A solitary night is perfect for watching this film.

From 1999 to 2014 (2 years ago), then to 2025 (9 years later), it's like being enveloped in the movie, with past memories surging to the surface.

A lifetime really is just a moment. Such a brief moment, yet it's filled with so many flavors. It's daunting to savor yet often lingers in memory.

Someday, will I also return to the mountains and rivers and see old friends again?
