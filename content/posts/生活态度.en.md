The baby finally fell asleep, so now there's a moment of peace.

Saw on Google Reader that Teacher Pi got a new Land Rover. Kinda hit me hard, but at the same time, I often feel that compared to some friends, I'm doing alright.

When will I learn to live "without comparing myself to others"?

When will I have enough space inside to embrace the outside world? To not feel inferior even in poverty next to those who have more, and to not feel boastful even in wealth next to those who have less.

In life, I hope I can find a calmness and composure.
