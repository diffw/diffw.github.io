The baby is finally asleep, giving me a moment of quiet.

I read on Google Reader that Mr. Pi got a new Land Rover SUV, which hit me quite a bit. At the same time, I often feel that, compared to some friends, I'm doing quite well.

When will I learn to live "without comparing myself to others"?

When will my heart have enough room to embrace the outside world?

Even in poverty, in the presence of the affluent, I hope not to feel inferior; even in wealth, in the presence of the deprived, not to feel proud.

In life, I hope to maintain a sense of calm and poise.
