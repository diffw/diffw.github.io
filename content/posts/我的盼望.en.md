Singing hymns early this morning: my hope is in Jesus Christ...

Lately, I've been thinking about some "hopes" (and worries): challenges at work, confusion about career development, Eather's elementary school (very demanding), learning to drive, and so on. But honestly, these aren't hopes placed in Jesus Christ. Deep down, I know that if they're not in Jesus Christ, there's no peace.

I need to turn back and ask the Lord for help.
