Early morning hymn: my hope is in Jesus Christ...
Some recent "hopes" have been running through my mind. However, these hopes (and worries), such as challenges at work, confusion about career development, Elim's demanding elementary school, learning to drive, etc., are actually not in Jesus Christ. In my heart, I know that without Jesus Christ, there's no peace.
I need to turn back and ask the Lord for help.
