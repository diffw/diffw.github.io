Open the bathroom door, step out — a cool draft. Autumn's here. Snuck up on me.

Moved into the new place. The commute's almost an hour and a half each way. Tiring, but I can take it. Plenty of "programming" along the way, too.

Right now, my wife and kids are asleep. I'm alone, standing in a 1999 apartment in Xuhui District, typing this. Cool wind outside. Crickets.

> He has made everything beautiful in its time. He has also set eternity in the human heart; yet no one can fathom what God has done from beginning to end.
> Ecclesiastes 3:11, NIV
