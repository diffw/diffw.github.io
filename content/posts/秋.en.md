Stepping out of the bathroom, a chill hits. Autumn has arrived, almost unnoticed.

We've moved to a new place, and the commute now takes nearly an hour and a half each way. Though it's tiring, it's manageable, and the "programming" during the journey is quite varied.

At this moment, my wife and child are asleep, and I stand alone in a 1999 apartment in Xuhui District, typing these words. Outside the window, there's a cool breeze and the sound of cicadas.

> He has made everything beautiful in its time. He has also set eternity in the hearts of men; yet they cannot fathom what God has done from beginning to end.
>
> Ecclesiastes 3:11
