In a dream, I was climbing a steep mountain with companions, and the steep side was the path to life. Nearly at the summit, I suddenly looked back for some reason. Seeing the sheer cliffs made me tremble with fear, leaving me stuck between going up or down.

"Forget what is behind, strive for what lies ahead." This phrase from the Bible came to mind.

I accepted it, stopped looking back, and focused solely on moving forward.

It was a dream.

Does it have anything to do with my current faith, life, or work?
