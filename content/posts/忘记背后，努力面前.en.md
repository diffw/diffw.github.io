In the dream, I was climbing with companions. A steep, steep side was the way to life. 

Almost at the peak, for some reason, I suddenly looked back. The cliffs made my heart tremble. I was stuck.

'Forget what lies behind, and strain toward what lies ahead.' This Bible verse came to mind.

I took it to heart. I stopped looking back, focused only on moving forward.

It was a dream.

Does it relate to my current faith, life, or work?
