The rooftop on the 17th floor of the company, though rarely visited, has accumulated many shoe prints, scratches from hard objects, and dried traces from rain over time... These patterns are random, sometimes orderly, sometimes chaotic, and mesmerizing... One day, I deliberately used a digital camera to capture these. I randomly picked one of the images, played around with the existing Hulitu image, and created this desktop wallpaper.

I hope you like it.
