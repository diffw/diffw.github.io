The company rooftop on the 17th floor, though rarely visited, over time has gathered all kinds of shoe prints, scratches, and marks left by rainwater drying... These patterns are both random and orderly, without order, and mysterious... One day I deliberately went up with a digital camera to capture them... Picked one at random, mixed it with the already existing Hulitu image, and voilà, this wallpaper was born.

Hope you like it.
