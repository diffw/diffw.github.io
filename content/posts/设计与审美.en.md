Because I believe:
1. In design, anything is possible;  
2. There is definitely more than one way to solve a problem;  
3. The plan at hand is not necessarily the most perfect;  
Therefore,  
Each time, I willingly accept the opinions and suggestions of team leaders or colleagues. Indeed, repeated revisions can elevate my design to a new height. But at the same time, with so many voices in my head, I gradually lose my ability to judge for myself, or what can be called the ability to appreciate aesthetics, the ability to choose the best among the best.

This is a very frightening thing.
