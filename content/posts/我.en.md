I tend to get emotional without realizing it, finding people annoying and situations uncomfortable. I admit, it's a bad habit.

My mom returned to Changsha today, after working hard for nearly 80 days. She said, "Your temper has become more eccentric; you weren't like this before." Indeed, over the years, without noticing, I've become quite irritable.

What to do?

Another thing I need to confess to my friends is that my faith situation is quite poor. I keep telling this person "believe," and that person "believe." But when it comes to God, there's always something in my heart that I can't overcome. I think mainly these:

1. Did Jesus really come to this world? Did He really resurrect from the dead? What does His birth, death, and resurrection have to do with me? Right now, I really can't feel it.
2. About prayer. How to pray... ............ .............. .. . . . . . . . . I can't explain it clearly either, and I don't know what the problem is.......
