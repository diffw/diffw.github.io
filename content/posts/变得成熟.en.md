I believe the challenge of living isn't about gaining more externally, but maturing internally as we age.

What is maturity? Last night, during our study of Romans, Teacher Han summed it up:

> Loving those who are "unlovable" or hard to love, submitting to those you can't submit to, and enduring people or things you find unbearable.

This morning, while playing basketball, the word "mature" suddenly came to mind, and I thought a lot about it.
