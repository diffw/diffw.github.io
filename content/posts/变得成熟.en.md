I believe that the challenge of living is not about becoming more materially abundant, but about becoming more mature internally as the years go by.

So what is maturity? During our Romans study last night, Teacher Han summarized it as:

> Loving those who are 'unlovable,' difficult to love, submitting to those whom it's hard to submit, and enduring people or things that are difficult to endure.

This morning while playing basketball, the word 'mature' suddenly came to mind, and many thoughts followed.
