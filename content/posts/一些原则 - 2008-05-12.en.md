Managing time wisely is more important than just saving it.

During work hours, avoid browsing the internet idly, including sites like Douban, Zhua Xia, Blue Idea, ffffound, and friends' blogs.

Try to make things, including work-related tasks, more interesting and cherish every opportunity to engage with graphics and colors.

Even if not yet successful, success feels like continually overcoming oneself.
