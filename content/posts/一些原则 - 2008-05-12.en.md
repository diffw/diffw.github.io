It's more important to manage time well than to simply save it.

During work hours, avoid browsing the web aimlessly. That includes Douban, Grab a Shrimp, Blue Ideal, ffffound, and friends’ blogs.

Make things interesting, even work-related tasks. Cherish each chance to engage with graphics and color.

Though I haven't succeeded, I feel like success is about continuously overcoming oneself.
