I hope that 100 instances of unconscious, free-form doodling can provide inspiration and technical support for one instance of [conscious, goal-oriented creation and design](http://www.dearbrant.com/2012/04/12/think_about_icon_design/). This is the primary purpose of my frequent doodling.

Beyond that, doodling for me is also a great way to vent and express emotions.

--

Within the industry, some hold the view: "Design has no direct relationship with art" and even, "Design can be entirely realized through computation."...

My view is: Good design inherently possesses beauty, whether it is an intuitive design or a rational one. A designer who lacks aesthetic sensibility is not a good designer.

How to improve aesthetic sense? Drawing is an excellent way.
