I hope that 100 sessions of aimless, scattered doodling can inspire and support me in one deliberate, goal-oriented creation or design. That's why I often doodle.

Besides, for me, doodling is a great way to vent and express emotions.

---

Some in the field think "design has no direct relation to art," and even assert that "design can be completely achieved through calculation"...

My view is: good design is inherently aesthetic, whether it's an emotional design or a rational one. A designer lacking in aesthetic sense isn't a good designer.

How to enhance this sense of beauty? Drawing is a good method.
