The exhaustion of the body, the emptiness of the soul, the lustful inclinations, the alienation in the marital relationship, uncontrollable anger towards my daughter Elim, the longing and anxiety for financial enjoyment, and the lack of God's presence when I try to pray. This sums up the current state of my life.

There is no joy in my heart, only darkness. The family atmosphere is tense, with outbursts of anger occurring frequently.

Lord, save me!

> I said to the Lord, "You are my Lord; apart from you I have no good thing."
> Psalm 16:2
