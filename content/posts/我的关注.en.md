Just now, while I was writing the previous post, Misa suddenly appeared behind me, saying they'd be distributing company stock to employees soon and emphasized the stock's value.

Honestly, my heart skipped a beat. It's about benefit, after all. I even wondered deep down how much I might get.

But then I came to my senses. As a designer, what's most important? What should I focus on?

I'm increasingly convinced that understanding design better and designing with ease is what I need to focus on. As for those benefits, they're just things that come along the way. A bit of patience is all that's needed to wait for them.
