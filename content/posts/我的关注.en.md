Just a moment ago, while I was writing my previous post, Misa suddenly appeared behind me and mentioned that in the next couple of days, the company would distribute stocks to the employees, emphasizing their value.

To be honest, I felt a twinge of interest. After all, this is about benefits. For a brief moment, I even wondered how much I would receive.

Fortunately, I quickly came to my senses. For a designer, what is most important? What should I focus on?

I am increasingly convinced that understanding design better and becoming more fluent in it is what I should prioritize.

As for those benefits, they are merely a side effect that requires a bit of patience to await.
