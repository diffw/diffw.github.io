I think only God can keep me away from infidelity. I've tried overcoming it with my own willpower many times and failed, leaving me feeling frustrated.

In the morning, I habitually scroll through my WeChat Moments. But after just a page, my thoughts turn to God. I want to offer the earliest and most precious part of my day to Him. So, I start with praise and devotional time.

During this time, I pray for my "lust of the eyes" and "inner impurity." Thank God, on the way to work, I'm not bound by "impurity" and feel so free and at peace.

Also, I'm thankful that what usually takes at least 15 minutes to walk from the "Dalian Road" subway station to my office, only took 5 minutes using a Mobile bike, saving me a lot of time.

> 'Bring the best of the firstfruits of your soil to the house of the Lord your God.'
> (Exodus 23:19, NIV)
