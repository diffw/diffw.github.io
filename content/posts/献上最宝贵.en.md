I believe only God can keep me from lust. I've tried several times to overcome it with my own willpower, but ultimately failed, leaving me with a strong sense of frustration.

In the early morning, I habitually browsed through my social media, but after just one page, my thoughts turned to God. I decided to dedicate the first and most precious part of my day to Him. So, I began to praise and engage in spiritual exercises.

During this time, I prayed for my "lust of the eyes" and "inner lust." Thank the Lord, on my way to work, I wasn't bound by "lust," and felt incredibly free and at peace.

Additionally, I'm grateful that the walk from the "Dalian Road" metro station to my company usually took at least 15 minutes, but with a Mobile bike, it only took 5 minutes, saving me a lot of time.

> Bring the best of the firstfruits of your soil to the house of the Lord your God.  
> Exodus 23:19
