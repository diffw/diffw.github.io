Whether it's about being human or our mindset, simplicity is better than complexity.

The world tends to favor complexity: scheming, deceit, and... life is like a game of chess. Regarding "complexity," even with myriad strategies and machinations, it's difficult to avoid mistakes, and the complexity is endless.

But "simplicity" can counter many complexities, using stillness to move, and handling change with constancy.

So, I choose to keep it simple.

If someone wrongs me, let them; if someone deceives me, so be it.

At the very least, I believe I am always in God's hands, and no matter what, I am at peace.
