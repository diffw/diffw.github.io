Whether it's in life or mindset, simple is better than complicated.

People gravitate towards complexity: scheming, manipulation, deceit...  
Life is like chess. When it comes to complexity, even with meticulous planning and strategies, there's always room for oversight. Besides, complexity has no end.

But "simplicity"—it counters many with one, maintains calm in action, remains unchanged amidst all changes.

So, I'll keep it simple.

If someone wrongs me, let it be. If someone deceives me, let it be.  
At least I believe I am always in God's hands. No matter what, I'm safe.

Reflections on my recent state of life.
