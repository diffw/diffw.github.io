In the city's summer nights, I miss the countryside sky from my childhood.
Back then, the nights were hot too, but there was a breeze.
On the dark blue curtain, stars twinkled.
But sometimes, it was fireflies that were flickering.
The night was purely dark.
Unlike the city's noisy lights.
The croaking of frogs, accompanied by the scent of fruits, lulled me to sleep;
Occasionally, the crickets in the corner would join the fun.
These are memories from before I was 15.
Later, there were fewer days spent at home.
Having lived in the city for so long,
But I'm still a farmer;
I would love to be a farmer;
Someday, I will truly return home to farm.
I love the countryside.
Today is my mom's birthday;
I'm feeling a bit homesick.
There's a carrot spacecraft,
HT 07;
I quietly climbed aboard;
Ready to return to my hometown.
Goodnight, everyone.
