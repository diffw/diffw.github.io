Philippians 4:10-14

I rejoice greatly in the Lord that now at last your concern for me has flourished again; indeed, you have always been concerned, but you had no opportunity to show it. Not that I speak from want, for I have learned to be content in whatever circumstances I find myself. I know how to live humbly, and I know how to live in prosperity. Whether I am well-fed or hungry, whether in abundance or in need, I have learned the secret of being content in any situation. I can do all things through Him who gives me strength.
