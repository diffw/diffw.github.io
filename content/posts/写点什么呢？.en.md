I've opened the WP dashboard several times, always wanting to jot down something. When it comes to design ideas, they always feel shallow, scattered, and not worth sharing. As for life, it's just the usual stuff; too much chatter about it becomes dull. 

I should write something, at least to let my friends who care about me know that I'm alive and doing well.

1. I've changed jobs to Mammoth Technology. Honestly, it's a company that excites me. Although everything is still not perfect, every beginning has its hardships. I'm patient and prepared for this.

2. The work is very busy. Although a lot of the busyness feels wasted, it's the price we pay for team cohesion, growth, and the standardization of workflow.

3. The passion is still there. Last Friday, I told Misa I wanted to redesign the company logo, as the current one has many issues. Over Saturday and Sunday (church service remained unchanged on Sunday morning), I worked tirelessly and submitted a brand new logo proposal on Monday, complete with applications like business cards, product packaging, and system interface, and I printed new business cards. Cutting the cards myself and holding them in my hands brought immense satisfaction. The thrill of design.

4. Because the company is expanding rapidly, I've been pondering the issue of team building. Even though I'm not in that position, I hope Mammoth does well. For the entire team, my hope is: modest demeanor, commanding products. For the design team, I expect: passion, atmosphere, and motivation in design.

5. Even though there are many opportunities at the company, I've made it clear that the boss isn't the center of my work; God is.

6. On May 17th, my wife and baby went back to our hometown in Changsha for two months. During this time, I need to plan things well.
