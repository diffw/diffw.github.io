Invest yourself and focus on doing every important task!
Whether it's work or creative projects during your leisure time.
Additionally, I've finally realized that creating something also requires a thematic direction;
I never thought there could be a story between cherries and strawberries,
this is all thanks to imagination.
Set a small direction, a tiny theme, and then let endless imagination take over;
boundless imagination brings confusion and fear of the edge and the infinite, which was a mistake I used to make.
Don't be too greedy, just take it easy at heart.
