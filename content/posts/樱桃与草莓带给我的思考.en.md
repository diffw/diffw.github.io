Focus on doing every important task with dedication!
Whether it's work or side projects.

Also, I've finally realized that creating requires a theme.
I never thought a story could exist between cherries and strawberries,
all thanks to imagination.
Set a small direction, a small theme, and then imagine infinitely;
Boundless imagination leads to confusion and a fear of the limitless. That's a mistake I used to make.
Don't be too greedy, just be content.
