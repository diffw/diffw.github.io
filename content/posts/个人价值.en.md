Work and entrepreneurship should merely be means to realize personal value, rather than becoming the goal themselves. 

To achieve personal value, the prerequisite is having a clear personal direction. All past, present, and future experiences are meant to bring oneself closer to this "direction."

Simply put, whether it's work or entrepreneurship at present, the tasks planned along one's personal direction should be accomplished one by one.

2012 is almost halfway through. Looking back at my plans from the beginning of the year, which ones am I currently working on?
