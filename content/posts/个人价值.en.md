Work and entrepreneurship should just be ways to realize personal value, not become the purpose themselves.

Realizing personal value starts with having a clear personal direction. Every experience from the past, present, and future should bring us closer to this "direction."

Simply put, whether it's work or entrepreneurship now, the tasks planned in your personal direction should be completed one by one.

2012 is almost halfway over. Looking back at your plans from the start of the year, which ones are you currently working on?
