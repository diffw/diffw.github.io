I came across this article on the blog of a stranger [Manbo](http://manboli.ccblog.net/archives/2009/33308.html), and was deeply moved by these two excerpts.  
  
[John Wesley's](http://baike.baidu.com/view/297926.htm) Theory of Prayer
Have you ever had a day when you fasted and prayed? When you earnestly begged at the throne of grace, prayed steadfastly, then finally mercy would arrive.
Give me one hundred preachers who fear nothing but sin and desire nothing but God, and such alone will shake the gates of hell and set up the kingdom of heaven upon earth. God does nothing except in response to our prayers. All that God does is in response to people’s prayers.
Christ has many tasks for us to do. Some are easy, others difficult. Some bring honor, others bring reproach. Some align with our natural inclinations and worldly interests, some oppose both... But Christ has given us the strength to do all the work.  
  
[Augustine's](http://baike.baidu.com/view/35058.htm) Prayer
Merciful Lord! In You I find life and knowledge. I completely entrust my body and soul to You. Lord, You are the journey’s end, the resting place along the way. You shelter me under Your wings, keeping my spirit from the worldly distractions that disturb it. Lord, grant this heart of mine—the wellspring of raging waves—to find rest in You.
Giver of all blessings, revive the spirit of unwavering faithfulness within me, harmonize my dispersed thoughts and powers in Your presence, and free my captive spirit. Behold! The Lord stands at the door of my heart and gently knocks. Let me open it, and invite You in to renew my spirit. For God is the source of life, the eternal radiant light, bestowing upon me Your grace according to Your will.
My God! You are the light of the blind, the strength of the weak. You are also the strength of the strong, the light of the radiant. Hear the deepest prayers of my soul. Lord, help us turn to You, seek You, for You have not forsaken Your creation as many have turned away from You. Turn us back to seek You because we know You live within us. When we repent and turn into Your embrace, we weep for straying away, yet You gently wipe away our tears. At that moment, we weep for joy because the Creator is recreating us and comforting us. Hear our prayer, Lord, that we may love You with all our heart. Lord, save us from eternal temptation. Amen!
God, blessed are Your saints. They have safely passed through the tempests of life, reaching spiritual joy and peace. Lord, look upon us with mercy as we journey through life's perils, remembering how we are tossed by trials and tribulations. Rescue us from all dangers. Lord, grant that our little vessel may safely reach the harbor we long for.
God, You are the light of the humble, the life of those who truly love You, the strength of those who sincerely seek You. Lord, let me be faithful to the end in Your holy love, rejoicing fully in You. Lord, let me offer all to You and invite You into my heart to dwell. I know how cramped my spirit is, insufficient to hold Your presence, but please expand it. Here all is broken ruins, but restore it, Lord. Where there is filth displeasing to You, whom but You can I beseech to cleanse me? I implore You to remove the hidden sins within me. Prevent Your servant from being arrogant, succumbing to evil.
