Yesterday I submitted a proposal to our company's boss, genuinely hoping the higher-ups would approve and support it. Although I have some skills and experience, such a large plan makes me feel unsure about my confidence and abilities. Still, I can see what Jiutian wants to accomplish. Someone has to do it, and since there's no one more suitable, I'll volunteer.

The Bible teaches me that if I focus on people or myself, my lack of ability and confidence will lead to loss of faith, especially when faced with difficulties. The inevitable result is feeling inadequate, scared, unable to move forward, and eventually, giving up. But if I fix my eyes on God, who created heaven and earth, everything changes. He has the power to create the universe, so what's a little website project? Moreover, He is willing to help and empower those who seek His assistance.

I keep reminding myself, in this matter, to focus solely on God, to pray and ask for His help.

For a project like this, I need a clear mind. But chasing fleeting glory or a tiny bit of power confuses my thoughts. I need more humility.

Give glory to God.

I encourage myself with Romans 8:31: "If God is for us, who can be against us?"
