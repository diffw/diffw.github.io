To be honest, I don't know how long I can keep this up, but indeed, I've started this lifestyle today.
5:30 AM. I thought it was quite early, but when I reached the entrance of my neighborhood, I found that many people were already busy—commuters, breakfast vendors, and even a couple of school kids. One of them looked about 10 years old.

Cycling, I reached the basketball court at Zhejiang University around 6:00 AM and began playing.

Along the way, the roads were wide, the air was crisp, and the sky was vast. A fleeting thought crossed my mind: Why is the world so big, yet everyone confines their entire life to a hundred-square-meter apartment and a few square meters of a car? And, they're so obsessed with it.

Around 7:00 AM, I finished exercising.

I went to a teacher's house for a quick shower and began my devotional life.

At about 8:40 AM, I left for work.

Feeling refreshed.
