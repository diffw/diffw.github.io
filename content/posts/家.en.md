This is my 12th move, and my 3rd move since living in Shanghai for 3 years. 
Every time I leave a place, I reflect on how that once warm and bustling home, where I used to come and go, suddenly becomes unfamiliar and unrelated to me; how "home" turns into just a "house" in an instant.
 
That's why I know very well that only where "family" is, is home. Thus, where we live, what kind of house we live in, or whether we rent or buy, none of that matters as much.
