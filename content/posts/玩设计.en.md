不是每个设计师都有“玩设计”的资本，但每个设计师都可以有“玩设计”的心态。

Not every designer has the luxury to "play with design," but every designer can embrace a playful mindset toward design.

别把设计当成工作，当成玩游戏，玩玩具吧，这样，设计师的内心会放松、宽广、谦卑、强大很多。

Don't treat design as a job; think of it as playing a game or a toy. This way, a designer's heart becomes much more relaxed, open, humble, and strong.
