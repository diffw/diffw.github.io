I've never been busier than I am now. But each time an idea becomes an actual design, each time the team recognizes a design, each time I contribute a small step in advancing the overall design, I feel satisfied and happy. Even if it means cycling five kilometers home late at night around ten.

At the same time, being busy has cost me some precious things. I don't have much time for Yanbing and the kids. I love you guys.

Today, I made eight-month-old Eather laugh for half an hour. That kind of happiness can't be replaced by design.
