I have never been as busy as I am now; however, whenever an idea becomes an actual design, whenever a design is recognized by the team, or whenever I contribute a small step toward the overall design advancement, I feel fulfilled and joyful. Even on nights when I cycle back home, five kilometers away, past ten o’clock.

However, being busy also means I lose some precious things. I don’t have much time to spend with my wife and baby. I love you both.

Today, my eight-month-old daughter Elim laughed heartily for half an hour because of my teasing. Such happiness cannot be replaced by design.
