Recently, I've been at a low point. I don't know how to be a person, a husband, or a father.

I'm tired, weary, confused, empty, and chaotic. My body is in pain, my eyes are dry, and I'm suffering from a sore throat due to a cold.

Self-pity.

I also pray, but they're weak prayers. It feels like talking to myself. Even if it seems like it's just a form of religious solace (that's how much I've belittled faith), it's still all I have.

I've also talked to my wife about some of my feelings, but ultimately, people can never fully understand or share each other's burdens.

People are truly lonely. (Thinking this way actually makes me wonder how anyone can go without knowing God?)
