Focusing is really difficult.
Whether it's for individuals or companies.

People want to acquire skills in a few days, or even hours, that others have honed over years.
They want to use a limited number of resources, in a limited amount of time, to create what a company, with hundreds of employees, has accumulated over years of product development. (At large companies, this even includes simultaneously "imitating" several products.)

The result is inevitably terrible!!!!

Understanding oneself is the most challenging task, whether for individuals or for companies.
