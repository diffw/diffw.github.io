Focus—it's really hard.

Whether for an individual or a company.

Wanting to acquire in a few days, or even a few hours, the skills that others hone over years;

Trying with just a few resources, within a limited time, to achieve what a company of hundreds accumulates over years with its products. (In big companies, it's often about trying to "imitate" several products at once.)

The result is inevitably terrible!!!!

Understanding yourself, whether for an individual or a company, is always the toughest task.
