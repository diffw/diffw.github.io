Many issues are worth reflecting on. However, it's important to think about the right issues at the right time. For instance, at home, consider family matters; at the office, focus on work-related issues...

Yet, I've always managed to confuse this correspondence. As a result, I often feel overwhelmed and busy with work while at home and find myself distracted by non-work matters when at the office.
