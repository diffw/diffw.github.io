A lot of things are worth thinking about.

But it's important to think about the right things at the right time.

For instance, at home, think about family matters, and at work, think about work issues, etc.

However, I've often mixed these up, 

which leads to feeling overwhelmed and busy at home with work pressure, and being distracted at the office, concerned with things outside of work.
