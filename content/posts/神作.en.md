Who is the most incredible designer? The Creator. Be it form, color, or composition—everything is alive, vibrant. What's even more amazing is the ability to create a human soul capable of independent thought. How marvelous.

I spend my days chasing after second-rate design works. I'd rather waste time online than get close to the greatest masterpiece.

Whether it's a plant or a small flower, how exquisite and lively they are.

I wonder, if a designer cannot discover, feel, and appreciate the grand beauty hidden in nature—the beauty of life—can they truly create good designs?

Perhaps there will be some so-called "designs," but they will not be "beautiful" at least.
