Who is the most amazing designer? The Creator. Whether it's form, color, or composition, everything is alive and vibrant. Even more astonishing is His ability to create human souls, each with an independent mind. How wonderful.

I spend my days chasing after second-rate designs, preferring to browse online rather than connect with the greatest works.

Whether it's a single plant or a small flower, how intricate and vivid they are.

I wonder, can a designer who can't discover, feel, and appreciate the grand beauty of nature—a beauty that includes life itself—really produce good designs? Maybe they can create some so-called "designs," but they certainly won't be "beautiful."
