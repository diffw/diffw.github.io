Today I did something wrong.
I saw a nail on the steps entering the company. To prevent others from stepping on it and getting hurt, I picked it up.

It was a brand new nail.

As I held it in my hand and walked a few steps, I thought about it and realized there wasn't much use in carrying it around. So, without much thought, I tossed it under the dark stairwell.

At the time, I didn't think much of it.

But when I returned in the evening, I suddenly realized how tragic that nail's fate was. In the prime of its life, it was destined to stay in the stairwell forever, until it rusted and was eventually thrown away as garbage.
