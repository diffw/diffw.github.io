It's been a long time since I recorded anything. The last entry was in 2023, and now it's already July 2024.

Life has been busy, chaotic, and I haven't felt like documenting anything. Life is also very ordinary; the trivialities of life and the ebb and flow of emotions don't seem worth recording.

Recently, while packing my bags, I came across a journal from ten years ago. Back then, I actually wrote a diary by hand. Flipping through a few pages, some past events resurfaced in my mind. But I didn't want to keep reading—not like opening a bottle of wine that hasn't fermented properly. It's not the right time to read them now; it should remain sealed.

However, this notebook made me reflect on the past six months of my life. I realized I've been in a state where, for the past six months, it's as if I've been dead. For me, a life without documentation is a life not lived.

Yet, there's also a deep sense of nihilism. Who cares about these insignificant words?

Currently, I'm going through a low point in my faith.
