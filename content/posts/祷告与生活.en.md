In the past, my prayers were often asking God to help me accomplish something. A lot of what I asked for was filled with worldly desires, vanity, and unholiness.

Today, I had a different feeling about prayer. Praying means trusting and relying on God, moving forward with faith, joy, and effort to do what needs to be done. Life isn't about achieving something but finding value in our existence through the things we do.
