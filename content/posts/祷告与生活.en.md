In the past, when praying, I often asked God to help me accomplish certain things. These requests often contained worldly desires, vanity, and unholiness.

Today, while praying, I felt moved in a different way. To pray and ask God is to trust and rely on Him, to have faith and joyfully and diligently do what should be done. A person’s life is not about achieving something, but about finding self-worth in doing something.
