For a product, visual expression that is detached from product structure is meaningless.

Visual expression is emotional, like an artist's "doodles," not "composition-creation"; 

Visual design balances rationality and emotion, built on the understanding and optimization of structure.

Visual expression is detailed and fragmented;

Visual design is a complete, coherent, logical, and structured solution.

Visual design solves problems through visual expression.

Of course, you need to figure out what problems to solve before starting your design. This requires product awareness and interaction awareness beyond visual expression.

If a visual designer cannot fully understand the product, lacks product awareness, or interaction awareness, then he cannot be considered a true visual designer.
