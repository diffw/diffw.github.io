Sitting on my electric scooter waiting for the light to change, I realized: having a family already established and stable is a huge advantage for me at this stage, no matter what I plan to do next.

So many young people are still struggling to "start a family" (often tied down by worldly notions like "having a car and a house")!

Thinking about this, I feel especially grateful!
