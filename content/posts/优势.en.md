Sitting on the electric scooter waiting at a red light, I realized:

Having established a stable family is a significant advantage for me at this stage, no matter what I plan to do next.

How many young people are still striving to "start a family"—many of whom are tied down by conventional notions like "owning a car and a house"!

Thinking of this makes me incredibly grateful!
